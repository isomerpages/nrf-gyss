<p>The Global Young Scientists Summit brings together distinguished scientific
leaders and a new generation of research talent.</p>
<p>Anchored on the theme “Excite, Engage, Enable”, the 2026 Summit provided
a vibrant platform for fresh ideas, candid conversations and meaningful
collaboration.</p>
<p>Over five days, nominated participants experienced plenaries, panels,
fireside chats, quickfire pitches and poster sessions, and visited Singapore’s
leading research institutions and universities to get first-hand exposure
to cutting-edge science and technology.</p>
