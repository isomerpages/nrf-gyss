<p>Dr Cheong is Vice Provost for Strategic Research Partnerships in Singapore
Management University, Senior Adviser for Health Economics at Singapore's
Ministry of Health (MOH), and Chair of the Board Oversight Committee of
Advanced Cell Therapy and Research Institute of Singapore (ACTRIS). Previously,
as Deputy Secretary (Technology) at MOH, Dr Cheong deployed technology
against COVID-19, and developed the health research and innovation ecosystem
to achieve Healthier SG (i.e., population health, precision health and
preventive health).</p>

<p>Professor Lisa Ng is the current Executive Director of A*STAR's Infectious
Diseases Labs, where she manages the overall strategic direction of key
initiatives. She also serves as Executive Director of the Biomedical Research
Council, where she is involved with talent management and strategic initiatives.
Prof Ng is a SNAS (Singapore National Academy of Sciences) 2022 fellow,
and an exclusively elected member of the prestigious Henry Kunkel society
for immunologists of human diseases.</p>

<p>Professor Simon Chesterman is David Marshall Professor of Law and Vice
Provost (Educational Innovation) at the National University of Singapore
(NUS), where he is also the founding Dean of NUS College. He serves as
Senior Director of AI Governance at AI Singapore and Editor of the <em>Asian Journal of International Law</em>.
Previously, he was Dean of NUS Law from 2012 to 2022 and Co-President of
the Law Schools Global League from 2021 to 2023.</p>
